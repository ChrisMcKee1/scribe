# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Windows 11 users who dictate into everyday work applications and want speech input that is fast, dependable, private, and easy to inspect. They use Settings to tune behavior, understand performance, and recover from problems without becoming speech-recognition experts.

## Product Purpose

Scribe provides fully offline push-to-talk dictation that types polished text into the focused application. Success means dictation feels immediate, preserves user privacy, works consistently across applications, and makes optional AI cleanup transparent and controllable.

## Brand Personality

Private, fast, quietly delightful. The voice is plainspoken and technically credible, with moments of earned personality that make repeated use feel satisfying without turning work into a game.

## Anti-references

Avoid flashy consumer gamification, streak pressure, confetti, arbitrary scores, decorative SaaS dashboards, marketing-style cards, and dense enterprise telemetry that requires specialist knowledge. Do not sacrifice Windows familiarity for novelty.

## Design Principles

- Make privacy and offline operation evident through behavior, not repeated claims.
- Translate technical measurements into useful, human-readable signals.
- Reward real progress with personal records and honest local data, never invented scores.
- Preserve Windows-native interaction patterns and predictable controls.
- Keep primary workflows calm and fast while allowing deeper detail on demand.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast while following Windows system theme and accessibility settings. Preserve keyboard navigation, visible focus, readable scaling, color-independent meaning, and reduced-motion behavior. Avoid engagement patterns that punish breaks or pressure continued use.
